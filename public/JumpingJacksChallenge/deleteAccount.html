Delete Account — Jumping Jacks Trainer

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly from within the Jumping Jacks Trainer app:

  1. Open the app and go to Profile.
  2. Tap Delete Account.
  3. Confirm your choice. Once confirmed, your account will be permanently deleted.

What happens after deletion

  • Your account and all training progress stored in Firebase will be permanently deleted.
  • The deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.
  • We do not store a copy of your data and we do not offer a data export option.
  • Purchase records may still be retained by Google Play or the App Store for billing purposes. We do not have access to this information.

Need help?

If you experience any issues deleting your account, please contact us at dawikk.apps@gmail.com.
