Frequently Asked Questions

How does the adaptive difficulty system work?

+

Our system monitors your performance and adjusts puzzle difficulty in real-time. As you improve, you'll face more complex opening challenges. The rating system ensures you're always appropriately challenged.

Can I use the hint system without penalty?

+

The hint system is designed for learning. Using hints may slightly affect your rating gain, but the focus is on understanding opening principles rather than pure solving speed.

How does puzzle history help my improvement?

+

Puzzle history lets you review past challenges, identify patterns in your mistakes, and track your progress over time. It's essential for understanding which opening themes you need to work on.

Are the puzzles based on real games?

+

Yes! Our puzzles are derived from master games and common opening positions. You'll practice scenarios you're likely to encounter in your own games.

What skill levels does the app support?

+

Chess Opening Tactics serves players from beginner to master level. The adaptive system ensures appropriate challenges for ratings from 800 to 2400+ ELO.
