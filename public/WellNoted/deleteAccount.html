Delete Account — WellNoted

How to delete your account

You can delete your account directly from within the WellNoted app:

  1. Open the app and go to Settings.
  2. Scroll down to the Actions section.
  3. Tap Log out, then contact us to request full account deletion.

Alternatively, you can request account deletion by emailing us directly.

What happens after deletion

  • Your account and all associated data (notebooks, notes, tasks) will be permanently deleted from our servers.
  • Any locally stored data on your device will remain until you uninstall the app.
  • The deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • We do not store a copy of your data after deletion and we do not offer a data export option through this process (you can export your notes from within the app before deletion).
  • Purchase records may still be retained by Google Play or the App Store for billing purposes. We do not have access to this information.

Need help?

If you experience any issues deleting your account or would like to request account deletion, please contact us at dawikk.apps@gmail.com.
